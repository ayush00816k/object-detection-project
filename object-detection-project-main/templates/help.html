    How to Use This System

    Upload Your Image

    Navigate to the "Live Upload" page and either drag-and-drop an image or click to browse your files. You may also capture an image using your webcam.

    Preview & Confirm

    Review the uploaded or captured image in the preview window. Ensure the object is clearly visible and well-centered for optimal detection results.

    Analyze Results

    Click "Analyze Image" — your image is processed by all 8 ML & DL models and results are displayed in an interactive bar graph.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    What types of images can I upload?
    You can upload JPG, PNG, or JPEG images up to 10MB. Use clear, well-lit images with a single prominent object. Models detect persons, animals, vehicles, furniture, electronics, food items, sports equipment, and tools.
    How does the comparative analysis work?
    Your image is processed through YOLO, CNN, ResNet-18, MobileNet, KNN, SVM, Decision Tree, and Random Forest. Each outputs a confidence score shown in a comparative bar chart.
    Which model is the most accurate?
    Deep learning models (YOLO, CNN, ResNet-18, MobileNet) achieve the highest accuracy. YOLO typically performs best due to advanced real-time detection.
    Can I use the webcam on mobile devices?
    Yes — works on modern mobile browsers. Allow camera permissions. If unavailable, use file upload instead.
    Is my uploaded data stored or shared?
    No — all processing happens locally in your browser. Images are never stored or uploaded.
    What do the accuracy percentages mean?
    Accuracy % = model confidence level. Scores above 85% are considered highly reliable.
    Why do different models show different accuracies?
    Different algorithms — DL models learn patterns, ML models rely on statistical methods. Accuracy differences reflect model strengths.
    Can I test multiple images in one session?
    Yes — after viewing results click “Test Another Image” and repeat as many times as you like.

    Troubleshooting

    Webcam not working

    Enable browser camera permissions, refresh page, and retry.

    Upload not responding

    Supported formats only (JPG, PNG under 10MB). Enable JavaScript.

    Low detection accuracy

    Use high-quality, clear images with a single centered object.

    Chart not displaying

    Refresh page — ensure browser supports modern web features.
